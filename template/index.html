Who We Are

Automovs will make your life easier by helping you to book your car servicing without even going to the maintenance person. You can book your servicing with the help of a few clicks and can experience great service and that too quite conveniently.

You can choose among a range of service options for your car and we make sure to provide you with services that are as per the recommendations of the company so that the right type of servicing can be provided to your car.

We cater our services in all of Kolkata and we are easy to get connected with the help of a number of service centers distributed throughout.

What are the services that we provide? We specialize in a range of servicing options such as repairs, denting, a/c heating in cars, cleaning, a pre-purchase inspection of the car and many others.

Our team makes sure that we can offer services to almost all types of cars from all types of brands and that too at a much affordable price.

Automovs was established in the year 2018 and since then we have constantly evolved with the help of the customers who are happy with us and who have shown their trust in us over time.
